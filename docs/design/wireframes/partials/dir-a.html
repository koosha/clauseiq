A · Strengths
  • Familiar — mirrors Westlaw, Lexis, Bloomberg Law mental model.
  • Filter sidebar always visible — low discoverability tax.
  • Dedicated contract viewer = URL, shareable, bookmarkable.
  • Clean back-button flow; easy to onboard.
A · Tradeoffs
  • Full-page navigation interrupts momentum on long sessions.
  • Scratchpad only partially visible from results; pinning feels less like a first-class action.
  • Filters consume 240px of real estate the user may not always need.
  • Feels less modern; closer to a portal than a tool.
